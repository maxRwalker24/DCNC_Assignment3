### Mental health resources and support for students.
### Mental health services
If you need mental health support, help is always available. Find out about
free and professional services available to you through RMIT or in the
[ ![A student smiles as she speaks to someone offcamera.](/content/dam/rmit/au/en/students/images/services-and-support/healthsafety-wellbeing/counselling/counselling-promo.jpg) ](/students/supportservices/health-safety-wellbeing/mental-health-counselling/counselling)
### [RMIT Counselling and Psychological Services ](/students/supportservices/health-safety-wellbeing/mental-health-counselling/counselling)
Free, confidential and professional short-term counselling for students
experiencing mental health issues including anxiety, depression and stress.

### [Safer Community ](/about/our-locations-and-facilities/facilities/safetysecurity/safer-community)
Safer Community is RMIT's dedicated service for students and staff, to seek
support about experiences of violence, sexual harm, concerning, unwanted,
uncomfortable or threatening behaviour.

University Library provides free study support services and resources to help

Additional support is available to ensure equal opportunities for students
with a disability, long-term illness or mental health condition, and primary
carers of individuals with a disability.
