## Who to call in an emergency
### In Australia
Service | Phone number
---|--Emergency services – Ambulance, Fire and Police | 000
[Campus Security](/about/our-locations-and-facilities/facilities/safety-security/campus-security) – Emergencies on RMIT Melbourne campuses | 9925 3333
Urgent mental health support | Phone 1300 305 737 Text 0488 884 162
### Outside Australia
Service | Phone number
---|--24/7 RMIT International SOS Phoneline - (for students on exchange) | +61 2 9372 2468
24/7 Health and Support Phoneline (Medibank) (for international students located offshore) | +61 2 8905 0307
[Australian Government Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade](https://www.dfat.gov.au/) | +61 2 6261 3305

### Safer Community - Threatening or unwanted behaviour including sexual harm
Phone +61 3 9925 2396
Email [safercommunity@rmit.edu.au](mailto:safercommunity@rmit.edu.au)
Safer Community checks emails and phone messages daily from Monday to Friday.
It is not an emergency service. In an emergency, phone 000, or if you are on
campus, [contact Campus Security](/about/our-locations-andfacilities/facilities/safety-security/campus-security).
[ Find out more about Safer Community
![](/etc.clientlibs/rmit/clientlibs/clientlib-webpack/resources/icons/rightarrow.svg) ](/about/our-locations-and-facilities/facilities/safetysecurity/safer-community)
### Public support services
These organisations are for the general public in Australia. They are not
managed by RMIT University.
#### Anxiety, depression or suicide
Service | Phone number
---|--[Lifeline](http://www.lifeline.org.au/) | 13 11 14
[Suicideline](http://www.suicideline.org.au/) | 1300 651 251
[Suicide Call Back Service](https://www.suicidecallbackservice.org.au/) | 1300 659 467
[OCD and Anxiety Helpline](http://www.arcvic.com.au/) | 1300 269 438
[Kids Helpline](https://kidshelpline.com.au/) (5 to 25-year-olds) | 1800 551 800
#### Sexual assault
Service | Phone number
---|--[Sexual Assault Crisis Line](http://www.sacl.com.au/) | 1800 806 292
[Centre Against Sexual Assault](https://www.casahouse.com.au/) | 9635 3610
[MensLine](https://mensline.org.au/) | 1300 789 978
#### Drugs, alcohol and gambling
Service | Phone number
---|--[Turning Point](https://www.turningpoint.org.au/) (Alcohol, drug and addiction support) | 1800 250 015
[Gambler's Help](https://gamblershelp.com.au/) | 1800 858 858
#### Grief and loss
Service | Phone number
---|--[The Compassionate Friends](https://www.compassionatefriendsvictoria.org.au/) | 1300 064 068
[GriefLine](https://griefline.org.au/) | 1300 845 745
#### Staff line for urgent support - Staff only
A dedicated phone line for RMIT Staff who have serious concerns about a
student's welfare or wellbeing and require timely assistance.
Service | Phone number
---|--[Staff line for urgent student support](/staff/service-connect/safety-wellbeing/supporting-students/staff-line-for-urgent-student-support) | 9925 1111

#### Acknowledgement of Country
RMIT University acknowledges the people of the Woi wurrung and Boon wurrung
language groups of the eastern Kulin Nation on whose unceded lands we conduct
the business of the University. RMIT University respectfully acknowledges
their Ancestors and Elders, past and present. RMIT also acknowledges the
Traditional Custodians and their Ancestors of the lands and waters across
Australia where we conduct our business - Artwork 'Sentient' by Hollie
Johnson, Gunaikurnai and Monero Ngarigo.
